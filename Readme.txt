## 💡 Project Overview

AssetFlow AI is an intelligent enterprise asset management platform that enables organizations to efficiently manage, assign, monitor, and maintain their assets. The system leverages AI-powered predictive maintenance, QR-based asset tracking, and real-time analytics to reduce operational costs and improve resource utilization.
